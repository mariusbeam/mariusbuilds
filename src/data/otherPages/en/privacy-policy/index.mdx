## What I collect and why

**When you use the contact form.** The form on the contact page is provided by Tally (Tally BVBA, Belgium). When you submit it, I collect the details you enter (such as your name, email, and message). I use these only to reply to you and to discuss working together. Legal basis: your consent and the steps taken at your request before any agreement (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a) and 6(1)(b)).

**Analytics.** I use Umami, a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It reports aggregate, anonymous stats (like page views and referrers) so I can see what's useful. No individual visitor is identified.

**Server logs.** My host (Netlify) processes basic technical data such as IP address and browser type to serve the site securely. Legal basis: legitimate interest in running and protecting the site (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

I do not sell your data, and I don't use it for advertising.

## Who processes your data on my behalf

- **Tally** (tally.so) — contact form submissions. EU-based.
- **Netlify** — website hosting and delivery.
- **Umami** — anonymous, cookieless analytics.

Each acts as a processor under a data processing agreement. Where any provider transfers data outside the EEA, that transfer relies on Standard Contractual Clauses or an equivalent safeguard.

## How long I keep it

Contact form messages: kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any resulting work, then deleted. Server logs: short retention set by the host. Analytics: aggregate only, no personal data retained.

To exercise any of these, email reach@beamlight.me and I'll respond within one month. You also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.
