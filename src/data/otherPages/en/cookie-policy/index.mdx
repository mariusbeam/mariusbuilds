_Last updated 14 June 2026_

This site uses very few cookies. Here's exactly what's set, why, and how to change your choice.

## What I use

**Strictly necessary.** A small cookie stores your cookie-consent choice so you're not asked on every page. The site also uses your browser's local storage to remember your light/dark theme preference. These don't track you and can't be switched off, since the site needs them to work as you asked.

**Analytics (optional).** Umami is privacy-friendly and cookieless, so it sets no cookies and identifies no one. The analytics toggle in the cookie settings is there for transparency and future-proofing.

**Contact form.** The contact page embeds a Tally form. It loads when you visit the page. If you use the form, Tally may set cookies needed for it to work and to prevent spam.

## Cookie details

| Name | Set by | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|------|--------|---------|------|----------|
| cc_cookie | This site (CookieConsent) | Remembers your consent choices | Necessary | ~6 months |
| theme preference | This site | Remembers light/dark mode (local storage, not a cookie) | Necessary | Until cleared |
| Tally cookies | tally.so | Form functionality and spam protection when you use the contact form | Third-party | See Tally's policy |

## Managing your choices

Use **Open cookie settings** above to accept, decline, or change optional cookies. You can also open **Cookie settings** in the site footer, or clear cookies in your browser. You can always reach me at reach@beamlight.me without using the form.
