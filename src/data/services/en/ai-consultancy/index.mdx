Most business owners don't need a hundred AI tools. They need one thing automated. The hard part is knowing where to start.

I do two things here:

Implementation. I'll set up Hermes (an AI assistant) on a VPS for you, give it a personality that matches your business, connect it to your tools, and show you how to use it. It runs on your own server so your data stays yours.

Guidance. If you're not sure what you need yet, we'll look at your business together. I'll tell you what would actually help, what's a waste of time, and what tool makes sense. No selling, just an engineer's opinion.

If you have a process that eats your time and you think AI might help, we'll figure out the simplest thing that works.
